Accomodation

We have arranged accommodation at the venue with a special rate (10 % off). Please let us know the details in the form, and we will book it for you. We will stay the whole weekend (maybe longer), and we would love for you to join us!
The venue lies in the South Moravia region, surrounded by beautiful wineries and lovely small towns like Mikulov, Lednice, and Valtice. It is a beautiful part of CR, which is definitely worth visiting, especially if you already are in the area.

Getting there

The closest airport in the Czech Republic is Brno (but there are only a few options) and Prague. Another option might be Wien in Austria or Bratislava in Slovakia. Let us know your choice of airport and we will try to find the best possible way for you to get to the venue.
